Who we are

ScotNest Ltd is a company of expert builders, based in the West Highlands of Scotland. We specialise in high quality new builds and renovations, combining traditional craftsmanship with the latest eco-friendly technology.

Our clients’ satisfaction is at the centre of everything that we do; we will work closely with you at every stage from initial planning through to completion. With our extensive experience in creating unique, bespoke homes, you can be assured that your dream home will become a reality.

Services we offer

Whether your project is a new build, a renovation or an extension of an existing property, modern or traditional in design, ScotNest Limited offers a complete package of services. From architects and craftsmen-builders, to decoration and landscaping, we provide everything you need to complete your project. We are fully dedicated to your requirements and will ensure that quality is maintained throughout every aspect of the build.

Expertise

Our team of experienced and highly skilled professionals will complete your project to the highest standards. Our focus is very much on craftsmanship, quality materials and a high standard of finish. We believe that it is this attention to detail that greatly enhances the appeal of a home and the enjoyment of living in it. Whether you are restoring an historical property or building a new house with state of the art systems we have the skills and resources on hand to make it possible.



Technology

We are committed to using environmentally friendly construction methods and eco-conscious solutions for heating, hot water and power. Our homes are specially designed to maximise energy efficiency and minimise carbon emissions. We combine efficient insulation and heat recovery systems with super-efficient heat pumps, solar panels and underfloor heating. These elements provide the ultimate in efficient energy use, whilst minimising running costs.
